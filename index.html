Technological University of the Philippines – Taguig

VISION: The Technological University of the Philippines shall be a premiere state university with recognized excellence in engineering and technology education at par with the leading universities in the ASEAN region.

I. Objective:
  • Identify common kitchen tools, equipment, and paraphernalia used in food preparation.
  • Describe the specific functions of various kitchen tools and equipment.
  • Classify cleaning tools and equipment according to their types and uses.
  • Demonstrate proper selection and use of appropriate kitchen and cleaning tools for specific culinary tasks.
  • Apply safety and maintenance practices when handling kitchen and cleaning equipment.
II. Subject Matter:

Appropriate Kitchen Tool, Equipment and Paraphernalia
Grade 7 and Grade 8

III. Materials:
  • Laptop
  • Projector
  • PowerPoint Presentation
IV. Procedures:
A. Preparation
  • Prayer: Ask a student to lead.
  • Greetings & Introduction: “Good Afternoon class! I’m Sir Dave, your teacher today.”
  • Classroom Management: Clean up and align chairs.
  • Attendance: Secretary checks and reports.
  • Rules: Respect, Participate, Keep clean and safe.
B. Motivation

Game: Tool Talk (Riddles about Spoon, Knife, Frying Pan, Pot)

C. Lesson Proper

Discussion on types of kitchen tools, equipment, and cleaning paraphernalia with student interaction.

  • Measuring Cups, Spoons, Knives
  • Kitchen Shears, Flipper, Can/Bottle Openers
  • Refrigerator, Blender, Auxiliary Equipment
  • Dishwasher, Vacuum Cleaner, Floor Squeegee, Brooms
D. Generalization

Understanding proper kitchen tool use improves safety, hygiene, and cooking efficiency.

E. Application – Group Activity

Groups categorize and explain tools: Preparation, Cooking, Cleaning, Storage.

V. Evaluation:

Choose the correct answer:

  1. What is recycling?
    Answer: C. Reusing materials to create something new
  2. What type of recycling uses waste materials from manufacturing?
    Answer: D. Internal recycling
  3. Example of internal recycling?
    Answer: C. Using spent grain mash as cattle feed
  4. External recycling is done by:
    Answer: C. Reclaiming materials from used products
  5. NOT a recyclable material?
    Answer: C. Banana peel
  6. Advantage of recycling?
    Answer: B. Conserves resources
  7. Material recyclable into a sculpture?
    Answer: C. Paper
  8. Tin can as pencil holder is example of:
    Answer: B. External recycling
  9. Why is plastic commonly recycled?
    Answer: C. It can be reused in many ways
  10. Which promotes recycling?
    Answer: C. Creating art from waste
VI. Assignment / Agreement:
  1. Think of five kitchen tools you commonly use at home.
  2. List each tool, its use, and material (metal, plastic, etc.).
  3. Be ready to present one tool for recitation.
